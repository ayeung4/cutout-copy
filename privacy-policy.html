Cutout Copy Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026

Cutout Copy is a Chrome extension that lets you remove the background from an image by using a right-click action.

What We Collect

Cutout Copy processes an image only when you choose one of the extension's right-click actions.

When you use the extension, the following data may be sent to the Cutout Copy backend:

  • the image you selected or the image URL
  • the webpage domain where the action was used
  • the image domain
  • whether you chose copy or download
  • processing status, timing, and error information

Cutout Copy does not intentionally collect passwords, payment information, form contents, browsing history, or the contents of pages unrelated to the selected image action.

How Images Are Processed

Images are sent to the Cutout Copy backend and then to remove.bg for background removal. The processed transparent PNG is returned to your browser for copying or downloading.

Cutout Copy does not store image files in its current beta implementation. Operational logs may include metadata such as source domains, action type, timing, success/failure status, and output size.

How We Use Data

We use collected data to provide background removal, debug errors, understand copy vs download usage, improve reliability and performance, and monitor abuse and service cost.

Third-Party Processing

Cutout Copy uses remove.bg to perform background removal. Images submitted through the extension are processed by remove.bg according to its own terms and privacy practices.

Data Retention

The beta backend stores usage and event metadata for product analytics and troubleshooting. Image files are not stored by the Cutout Copy backend in the current implementation.

User Choices

You can stop using Cutout Copy at any time by disabling or uninstalling the extension in Chrome.
